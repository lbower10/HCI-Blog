Project 3: The Smart Snacker

Project 3 instructed us to create a device that utilized the IMU device provided to us in our electronics kit. This was our idea:

The Smart Snacker

The Smart Snacker is a device that utlizes the IMU to detect when our the snack box is about to top over, which normally would result in spilling your snacks everywhere. The Smart Snacker tackles this problem by closing a slide door over the opening to prevent snack spilling.

Preliminary Design

The trap door slides over the box opening via gears attached to chains, powered by motors that move the chains.
