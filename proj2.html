Project 2: Two-Player Color Matching

Project 2 instructed us to be creative and design our own primary input device. After brainstorming/pondering what device we might make, we settled on using conductive Play-Dough for obtaining user input.

The Game

The main idea of our game is for players to guess each others' input correctly. The arduino and other components will sit in a shoebox, or some other kind of encasing, with a divider in the middle and onnings on either side. These onnings will shield the conductive Play-Dough inputs from a player's opponent. Player 1 will press one of his input devices, which will be either red, green, or blue Play-Dough. It is then Player 2's job to correectly match Player 1's input. A player will score if their opponent does not guess correctly OR if they correctly guess what their opponent selected.
